Directions

Currently, the Hampton Roads Planning District Commission (HRPDC) staff supports the HRTA. HRTA meetings are currently being held in the Regional Board Room adjacent to the HRPDC offices in the HRPDC Regional Building.

The HRPDC Regional Building is located off Interstate 64 in Chesapeake. Take I-64 to Exit 289A, North Greenbrier Parkway. Proceed to the first traffic light and turn right onto Woodlake Drive. Turn into the first driveway on the right, just after the Hampton Inn, at the sign for the Regional Building. Take an immediate left into the parking lot.

 

The HRTA street address is:

HRTA

The Regional Building

723 Woodlake Drive

Chesapeake, VA 23320
