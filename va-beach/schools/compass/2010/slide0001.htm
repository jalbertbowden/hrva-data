Compass to 2015:
A Strategic Plan for Student Success
Presentation to the School Board
June 22, 2010
Year-End Report
Good evening Chairman Edwards, Vice-Chair Brunke and Members of the School Board,

Thank you for this opportunity to present you with a year-end progress report on Compass to 2015. I would like to begin with noting that I appreciate the Board’s commitment to our strategic plan. In my long career, I have never seen a policymaking body so single-minded in its support for the direction of its organization. That commitment has paved the way for some real forward momentum. Hopefully, I have done a good job keeping you updated on that momentum through my monthly Superintendent’s Reports, but tonight offers you an opportunity to get a holistic view of what has been accomplished and what we will be focusing on in year two of implementation.
