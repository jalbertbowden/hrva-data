This slide outlines the initiatives completed or ongoing aimed at addressing SO1.

Effectively illustrating how the 21st century skills apply to specific developmental levels and content areas and providing resources that support the infusion of the skills into the curriculum has required an unprecedented professional development effort.  To that end, we have scheduled mandatory training opportunities for all instructional staff throughout the summer that is tailored to each teacher’s teaching assignment.  The goal is for all of our teachers to have received this training before school starts in the fall.

SO1 in collaboration with SO2 has done much work in preparation for these training opportunities.  They include, but are not limited to, (a) curriculum revision aimed at emphasizing 21st century skills in each content area, (b) the development of the continuum of 21st century skills acquisition, which is an instructional planning tool designed to help teachers plan differentiated instruction and assessments emphasizing the 21st century skills, and (c) reconfiguring SchoolNet, the instructional management software utilized by the division, to allow for the inclusion of 21st century skills in the curriculum and provide teachers access to exemplary learning plans and assessments that will be used as models as they plan for high quality 21st century instruction.
