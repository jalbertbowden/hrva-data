BUDGET COMMENTS 

 

Funding has been provided for three New Town Special events.  A scheduled replacement vehicle is also 
funded in FY2014.  New spinning classes will be offered at the James City County Recreation Center and 
both  the spending and revenues associated with that program are reflected in this budget. Replacement 
picnic tables and jon boats are also included. Part Time temporary hours have been added to assist in 
administrative duties, such as revenue collection.
