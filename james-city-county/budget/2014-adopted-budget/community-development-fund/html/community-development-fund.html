BUDGET COMMENTS 

 

The FY2014 Community Development Fund Budget makes available funds for continued operation of the 
County-wide low-and moderate-income housing programs and neighborhood revitalization projects. Not 
included in the adopted budget is the appropriation for the Neighbor’s Drive CDBG project as the timing 
of the acceptance of the award is not until the summer of 2014.  At that time, a separate award and 
appropriation will be brought to the Board.  
 
Also included in program spending is the continuation of the annual $60,000 allocation to support 
Housing Partnerships, Inc. (HPI) as this office will continue to work with HPI to target these dollars to 
James City County homes in need. 
 
Not included in these budgets are Federal and State loan and grant funds that are provided to first-time 
homebuyers though the County’s participation in the Hampton Roads Regional Loan Fund Partnership.
